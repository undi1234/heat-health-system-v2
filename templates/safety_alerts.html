⚠️ Heat-Related Illness Symptoms

Watch for these symptoms and report immediately:

  • Dizziness or fainting
  • Excessive sweating or no sweating with hot, dry skin
  • Rapid heartbeat
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Muscle cramps
  • Headache or confusion

🚨 Emergency Contact

If you or someone you know is experiencing severe symptoms, call emergency services immediately or go to the nearest health center.
